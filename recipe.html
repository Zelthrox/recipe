Basic Mashed Potatoes

Prep 15minutes

Cook 20minutes

Total 35minutes

Ingredients

Click on ingredient to mark bought!
  • 2 pounds baking potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm, about 15minutes; drain
  2. In a small saucepan heat butter and milk over low heat until butter is melted. Using a potato masher or electric beater, slowly blend milk mixture into potatoes until smooth and creemy. Season with salt and peper to taste
